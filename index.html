About Us

Built to open African commodities to the world

Stratum Commodities exists to give extraordinary producers in underserved regions a direct path to European buyers. For too long, families growing, harvesting, and processing world-class commodities across East Africa have been held back by brokers, middlemen, and infrastructure gaps that swallow margin and drown quality in noise.

We started Stratum to cut that chain. We are the commercial and operational arm for a small network of family-owned facilities and trusted partners across the Horn of Africa. These are relationships built on decades of trust, shared heritage, and mutual accountability, not procurement spreadsheets.

Because we own the relationship from source to delivery, our partners reach European and global markets with their product intact: verified quality, genuine pricing, and a supply chain you can actually trust.

Flagship Product

Premium African Salt

Three grades of premium salt from our ISO 22000:2018 certified facility, independently verified by Bureau Veritas and Fera Science (UKAS accredited). Tested at 98.78% NaCl purity with all heavy metals below detection limits.

Iodised Salt

Food Grade

Refined iodized table salt, KI fortified with anti-caking agents. Fully compliant with KS EAS 35:2021 edible salt standards.

  • NaCl: 97 - 99.9%
  • Iodine: 30 - 60 ppm KI
  • Grades 1 - 3 (0.3mm to 2.0mm)
  • Retail pouches and 50kg sacks

Industrial Salt

Bulk Supply

High-volume salt for water treatment, de-icing, chemical processing, and industrial applications.

  • NaCl: 95%+
  • Bulk, 25kg bags, or jumbo sacks
  • Flexible specifications
  • Consistent supply guaranteed

Organic Salt

Organic / Natural

Solar-evaporated, additive-free rock salt with a rich natural mineral profile. No iodine, no anti-caking agents.

  • NaCl: 97 - 99.9% (dry basis)
  • 1.9x more calcium than Himalayan salt
  • 1.6x more magnesium than Himalayan salt, significantly lower contaminants
  • UKAS lab verified (Fera Science, UK)
