About Us

Built on Direct Relationships, Not Brokered Deals

Stratum Commodities Ltd is a UK-registered commodity trading company established to connect premium African raw materials with European buyers.

Unlike traditional brokers, we work directly with family-owned production facilities and trusted partners across East Africa. These are relationships built on decades of trust, shared heritage, and mutual accountability.

Because we control the supply chain from source to delivery, we eliminate the markups and inefficiencies that come with intermediaries. The result is genuinely competitive pricing on independently verified, high-quality products.

We are the trading and outreach arm for a network of family businesses and close associates, each specialising in a specific commodity. This gives us deep expertise and reliable supply across multiple product lines.

Flagship Product

Premium African Salt

Three grades of premium salt from our ISO 22000:2018 certified facility, independently verified by Bureau Veritas and Fera Science (UKAS accredited). Tested at 98.78% NaCl purity with all heavy metals below detection limits.

Iodised Salt

Food Grade

Refined iodized table salt, KI fortified with anti-caking agents. Fully compliant with KS EAS 35:2021 edible salt standards.

  • NaCl: 97 - 99.9%
  • Iodine: 30 - 60 ppm KI
  • Grades 1 - 3 (0.3mm to 2.0mm)
  • Retail pouches and 50kg sacks

Industrial Salt

Bulk Supply

High-volume salt for water treatment, de-icing, chemical processing, and industrial applications.

  • NaCl: 95%+
  • Bulk, 25kg bags, or jumbo sacks
  • Flexible specifications
  • Consistent supply guaranteed

Organic Salt

Organic / Natural

Solar-evaporated, additive-free rock salt with a rich natural mineral profile. No iodine, no anti-caking agents.

  • NaCl: 97 - 99.9% (dry basis)
  • 1.9x more calcium than Himalayan salt
  • 1.6x more magnesium than Himalayan salt, significantly lower contaminants
  • UKAS lab verified (Fera Science, UK)
